# Food Bridge
## A food waste redistribution and surplus routing platform

Restauraunts, grocery stores, food distributors, caterers, and even households often generate surplus food due to unsold inventory, over preperation or event leftovers. Much of this food remains safe
for consumption for a limited time but is frequently discarded
because there are no simple systems to share or redistribute it
efficiently. At the same time, community kitchens, shelters, food
banks, and individuals looking for affordable food may struggle to
access such resources, leading to a gap between available surplus
and potential recipients.

Thus,there is a need for a platform that allows food providers and
individuals to list surplus food that is still safe for consumption,
while enabling nearby buyers, community organizations, or
recipients to discover and claim it quickly before it expires.

## Our Solution
Develop a digital platform where restaurants, stores, caterers, and
individuals can list surplus food items with details such as
quantity, type, freshness window, and pickup location.<br>The system allows these items to be offered for discounted sale,
donation, or community redistribution<br>Enable users and
organizations to discover nearby listings, reserve items, and
coordinate pickups, helping reduce food waste and improve access
to usable food within local communities.
